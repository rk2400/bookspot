Agatha Christie


Agatha Christie wrote from the 1920s through the 1970s. Her books are almost entirely set among the British upper classes, and are commonly termed British cozy mysteries. Agatha Christie is not just a famous mystery writer, she’s the best selling novelist of all time with over two billion copies sold and a massive fandom that continues long after her death. But if you decide you’d like to see what Christie has to offer, with over 60 novels and 14 short story collections, getting started can be a daunting task.


Her Best Seller Books:

  • And Then There Were None
  • Murder on the Orient Express
  • The Murder of Roger Ackroyd
  • Death on the Nile
  • The ABC Murders

Robin Sharma


Robin Sharma is considered to be one of the top 5 leadership experts in the world. His work is embraced by rock stars, royalty, billionaires and many celebrity CEOs. He has sold more than 15 million books across 96+ nations, including the #1 bestsellers The Monk Who Sold His Ferrari book series.Sharma worked as a litigation lawyer until age 25, when he self-published MegaLiving, a book on stress management and spirituality. Sharma has published 12 other books, and founded the training company Sharma Leadership International.


His Best Seller Books:

  • The Monk Who Sold His Ferrari
  • The 5 AM Club
  • Who Will Cry When You Die?
  • The Leader Who Had No Title
